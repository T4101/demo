Industries We Serve

Forensic certainty matters across many sectors. We work with the organisations and individuals who depend on independent, defensible findings to make critical decisions.

Insurance Companies

Independent investigation and verification of claims linked to death, injury, fraud or disputed circumstances — supporting fair and accurate claims decisions.

Legal Firms

Expert reports, case review and court testimony for civil and criminal matters where forensic or medico-legal evidence is central to the case.

Government Agencies

Independent forensic consultation for departments requiring specialist input on death investigations, identification, or laboratory quality systems.

Law Enforcement

Scene investigation support, evidence recovery, and specialist consultation on cases requiring forensic anthropology, pathology or DNA expertise.

Laboratories

Quality assurance auditing, SOP development and accreditation support to help laboratories meet ISO 17025 / 17020 standards.

Mining Companies

Independent investigation of workplace fatalities and serious incidents, providing objective findings for internal review and regulatory reporting.

NGOs

Support for humanitarian and missing-persons initiatives, including forensic anthropology and human identification services.

Private Clients

Confidential, compassionate support for families seeking an independent review of a loved one's case — from death investigations to identification.
