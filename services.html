Our Process

How an engagement unfolds

A consistent process across every discipline, so you always know what to expect.

01

Confidential Enquiry

Share the background of your matter. We assess scope, urgency, and the right specialists for your case.

02

Engagement & Case File

A formal engagement is confirmed and a dedicated case file is opened under chain-of-custody protocols.

03

Examination

Our specialists carry out the analysis, review, or fieldwork required — in the lab, on scene, or on paper.

04

Report & Support

A written opinion is delivered, with consultation or testimony available for legal proceedings.
